Welcome to my website about my final year project!

Background

Social anxiety refers to a persistent, internalised fear of social and performance situations.
Social anxiety has affected employment rates and academic outcomes in young adults.
Rising cases globally justifies the need for low cost, scalable psychoeducational intervention.

Narrative Structure:

Visual novels are an interactive, narrative-driven video game with a focus on player choices.
Modified five-act narrative structure to add immersion and engagement.
Commonly featured in Western novels and games.

Theoretical Framework:

Psychoeducation framework is utilised to destigmatise and to educate players through narrative choice and perspective of story characters.
Creating empathy and increasing awareness of mental health conditions.

Future Applications:

Accessible mental health resource, targeted towards young adults.
Demonstrates the potential of visual novels as a serious educational tool.
Provides a foundation for personalised or adaptive digital experiences.
